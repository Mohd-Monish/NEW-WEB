ABOUT TECHFEST

Our Core Mission

Techfest was born in 1998 with the core motto of promoting science and technology and fostering genuine interest among students to solve real-life problems. We are the largest platform in Asia for youth to showcase their technical prowess.

Global Vision

We provide an international stage for students, academics, and industry leaders. Our events attract participants from over 2500+ Indian and 500+ international universities, ensuring a truly global exchange of ideas and innovation.

Year-Round Impact

Beyond the main event, Techfest drives year-round Social Initiatives (e.g., legal awareness, health drives) and the Ideate platform, connecting top innovators with corporations and government bodies for project implementation.

FLAGSHIP EVENTS
